About Us

We are Team 9189, the Rowville Roobots, the FRC team of Rowville Secondary College in Victoria. We are passionate about robotics and strive to excel in the FIRST Robotics Competition.

Our Team

Our team consists of dedicated students and mentors from Rowville Secondary College. We work together to design, build, and program our robots, and to develop teamwork and leadership skills.
